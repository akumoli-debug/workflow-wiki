## Key Insight So Far

In lending dashboards, the most useful KPI layout segments pipeline value by status rather than showing aggregate stats. The sales lead's question is "how much is stuck in Under Review?" — not "what's the total pipeline?" Aggregate stats require interaction to answer. Status-segmented cards answer on first paint.
